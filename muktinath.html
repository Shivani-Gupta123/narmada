7 Days Muktinath

Explore Muktinath with Jaiswal Tour Packages

Embark on a spiritual and scenic journey to Muktinath, Nepal, with Jaiswal Tour Packages. Nestled in the Mustang region, Muktinath is a sacred site revered by both Hindus and Buddhists, known for its divine temple and natural beauty. Our package offers a seamless travel experience, including comfortable transportation, accommodation, and guided tours. Marvel at the snow-capped peaks of the Annapurna range, cross serene valleys, and immerse yourself in local culture. Visit the 108 water spouts and experience the holy dip at the Mukti Kunda. This tour combines adventure and spirituality, making it ideal for families, solo travelers, and pilgrims. With us, you can relax and enjoy every moment, as we handle all the logistics. Explore Nepal’s unique charm with our thoughtfully curated Muktinath tour packages. Your spiritual journey awaits – book with Narmada tour & travels today!

Day 1 : Bihar - Lumbani - Pokhara - 315KM

Our journey will start from Bihar temple, located at Bihar, after our visit to Bihar railway station. We will commence to Sonauli which is 98 kms from Bihar from where we will head towards Lumbini, the birth place of Lord Buddha. Our journey to Pokhara will begin and upon reaching, we will halt there.

Day 2 : Pokhara Local Sightseeing

The 2nd day of our trip will start with a nice breakfast and energize our body for sightseeing. Starting off our day with a visit to Phewa lake, the biggest lake in Pokhara, we will also visit Talbarahi temple which is located at the center of Phewa lake. Our next destination is the oldest temple of Pokhara city known as Bindhyabasini temple.We will then head towards Seti river for the mesmerizing view, from where we will leave for The Devis Falls. The waterfall is sourced from Pardi Khola Stream, which eventually vanishes under the ground. We will then go to Gupteswar Mahadev Cave temple near Devis falls and end the day in a hotel in Pokhara.

Day 3 : Pokhara - Jomsom 180KM

Today, we will start our journey from Pokhara to Jomson, which is the gateway to the beautiful Mustang valley. The 180 kms drive towards Jomsom offers one of the best views you can get in Nepal. The road is filled with beautiful villages, apple orchards, pine trees, soothing waterfalls and beautiful snow capped mountains. Through the scenic route, we will reach Jomsom where we will stay in a hotel for a night.

Day 4 : Jomsom - Muktinath - Pokhara 205KM

Waking up early in the morning, we will be heading towards Muktinath temple, the ‘place of liberation(Moksh)’. After arriving at the Muktinath temple premises, we have a short 25 minute hike to reach the main temple. In the main temple, there are 108 water taps(“Dhara”) where you can take a bath and do a puja inside the temple. Upon that, you will get back to the jeep stand head towards Pokhara from the same route. We will spend a night in a hotel in Pokhara.

Day 5 : Pokhara - Manokamna - Kathmandu 205KM

On the 5th day of our trip, we will then head off to Kathmandu, where on the way we will visit Manokamna temple. After 105 kms drive from Manokamna temple, we will reach Kathmandu, and spend a night in a hotel.

Day 6 : Kathmandu Local Sightseeing

The day will start with a tasty breakfast and freshen up for sightseeing. We will start the day with a visit to Pashupatinath temple, located at Kathmandu, which holds great religious value to the Hindu community all over the world. We will then visit Guhjeshwari Temple, a holy temple situated at Kathmandu. We will continue our sightseeing excursion towards Buddhanath stupa, one of the largest spherical stupa in Nepal and towards Buddhanilkanth/Sleeping vishnu temple. Our last destination in Kathmandu is Swayambhunath. We can get a wonderful view of Kathmandu valley from Swayambhunath which is must-see tourist attraction in the heart of Kathmandu. We will wrap up for the day and stay in a hotel in Kathmandu.

Day 7 : Kathmandu - Bihar 370KM

On the last day of our tour, with all the sweet memories and experiences collected, we will be heading back to Bihar. On the way, we will visit Manokamna temple. At Bihar, everyone will be dropped off at railway station or airport.
